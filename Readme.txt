Code for the game XO game published on the play store.  
Xs and Os or Tic Tac Toeas known as some parts in the world, which was a paper-and-pencil game for two players. 
The X and Os, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3×3 grid. 
The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.  
Here we have created the same game as an app which you can play anytime and anywhere with your friends.
